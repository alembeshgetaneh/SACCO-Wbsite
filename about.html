About Maaddii SACCOs

Empowering members through savings, loans, and financial growth.

Our History

Maaddii SACCOs was established to provide reliable financial services for our local community. Starting with a small group of members, we have grown into a trusted cooperative supporting hundreds of families.

Mission & Vision

Mission: To improve the economic well-being of our members through affordable financial solutions and cooperative values.

Vision: To be a leading community SACCO that fosters sustainable growth and prosperity.
