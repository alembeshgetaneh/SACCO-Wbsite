Our Services

Maaddii SACCOs provides a variety of financial services to help our members save, borrow, and grow financially.

Savings Products

Secure and flexible savings options including voluntary, fixed, and compulsory deposits to grow your wealth.

Loan Products

Affordable loans for personal needs, business expansion, education, and emergencies with flexible repayment terms.

Share Capital & Dividends

Become a co-owner of the SACCO by buying shares and enjoy annual dividend distributions from profits.

Digital Banking

Access your savings and loans via mobile services and USSD to bank anytime, anywhere with ease.

Training & Advisory

We offer financial literacy, business guidance, and cooperative management training to empower members.

Membership Information

Joining Maaddii SACCOs is easy and rewarding. Find out if you qualify and what benefits await you.

Who Can Join

Any individual residing in the community who shares our values and commits to cooperative principles.

Benefits of Membership

  • Access to affordable loans and savings products
  • Participation in cooperative decision-making
  • Annual dividends from SACCO profits
  • Financial education and training opportunities

Requirements

  • Minimum age: 18 years
  • Membership application form submitted
  • Payment of compulsory shares
  • Agreement to SACCO bylaws and principles
